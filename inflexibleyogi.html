Inflexible Yogi Studio

Small Business Website Design

Project Overview

This is a group project, a team of four UX/UI designers. Inflexible Yogi Studio was established to help people manage stress and stay active. Due to covid restrictions the yoga studio could not accommodate usual amount of clients, which is affecting the business revenue. How might we help yoga studio owners to to stay in business by generating revenue and help retain existing clients during the Covid-19 Pandemic?


The Solution

Taking yoga practise digital will retain/gain customer base as well as keep them engaged and that we might be able to help if we come up with an online platform for their business. We might do this by designing a website that would include an online video library as well as live classes. Doing this will allow our product to be accessible to larger audiences as well as beneficial for the business.


Deliverables

UI style guide, information architecture /site map, sketches &wireframes, responsive high-fidelity mock-ups and webpages.
